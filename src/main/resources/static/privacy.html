Privacy Policy

How SlimPDF handles your data and documents.

Last updated: January 2025

1. Files You Upload

Any PDF you upload to SlimPDF is processed in memory for the purpose of compression and then discarded. We do not permanently store or share your documents.

2. No Accounts

SlimPDF does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the core compression feature.

3. Usage Analytics

We may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to measure traffic, popular pages and general performance. These tools help us improve the service but do not give us access to your PDF content.

4. Cookies & Ads

If advertising is enabled (e.g., Google AdSense), third-party vendors may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can manage ad personalization via your Google account settings.
